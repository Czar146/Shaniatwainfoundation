About US

The Shania Twain Foundation


The Shania Twain Foundation is a charitable singer-songerwriter Shania Twain.

The foundation focuses on supporting children's charities and organizations that promote education, health, and family welfare.

The Shania Twain Foundation has supported various initiatives:

1. Children's Miracle Network
2. Shania Kid's Can (SKC) - a program providing support to disadvantaged children in schools
3. Canadian charities like the Boys and Girls Clubs of Canadian
4. International organizations like the Red Cross


Shania Twain's Foundation aims to makes a positive impact on children's lives, reflecting her commitment to philanthropy and giving back to the community.
