The Eilleen regina twain Foundation


The Eilleen regina twain Foundation is a charitable singer-songerwriter Eilleen regina twain.

The foundation focuses on supporting children's charities and organizations that promote education, health, and family welfare.

The Eilleen regina twain Foundation has supported various initiatives:

1. Children's Miracle Network
2. Shania Kid's Can (SKC) - a program providing support to disadvantaged children in schools
3. Canadian charities like the Boys and Girls Clubs of Canadian
4. International organizations like the Red Cross


Eilleen regina twain Foundation aims to makes a positive impact on children's lives, reflecting her commitment to philanthropy and giving back to the community.
